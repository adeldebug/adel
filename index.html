About Me

Hello, I'm Adel Sibachir. Born on December 19, 2004, in Kouba, Algeria, I now live in Zeralda. Since 2022, I've been studying Computer Science at Houari Boumediene University in Algiers. Alongside my studies, I volunteer with the National Association of Algerian Volunteers and "Heart of Algeria for People with Disabilities," where I care for people with mobility impairments and participate in the "Caravan of Happiness." My motivation is to help others and make the world better. In the future, I want to work in IT and develop new technologies. I'm hardworking, friendly, and love learning. I speak Arabic as my native language, English at B2 level, French at B1 level, and I'm currently learning German. In my free time, I enjoy programming and IT.
